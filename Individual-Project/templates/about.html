Our Advice For You:

"My self-worth is not defined by the choices of others." Remember that you are in control of your own actions and decisions, and that you are valuable and worthy regardless of what others may think or say.

"I am strong enough to resist peer pressure." Believe in yourself and your ability to make the right choices for yourself. Trust yourself and be confident in your abilities.

"I will not compromise my values for the sake of fitting in." Stand up for what you believe in, and don't let peer pressure sway you away from your own values and principles.

"I am surrounded by people who support and encourage me." Surround yourself with positive influences and people who will uplift you and help you make the right choices

"I am not alone in this struggle." Remember that many people face peer pressure, and that it is a normal part of growing up. Remember that there is always help available if you need it, and you are not alone in your journey.
